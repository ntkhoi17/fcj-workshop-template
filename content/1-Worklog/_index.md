**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Manage operations, monitor, and move infrastructure on AWS](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [System and infrastructure management in the form of code and enhanced security on AWS](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Implementing the application, enhanced networking connectivity, and managing data on AWS](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Optimize costs and convert the Monolith application to Microservices](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Building and deploying a Serverless application on AWS](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Advanced Serverless Applications, Data Lake, and Data Analytics on AWS](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Event-driven architecture, high system readiness, and AWS security governance](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Doing task I...](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Doing task L...](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Doing task M...](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Doing task N...](1.12-week12/)
